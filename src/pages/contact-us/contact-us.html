About us in short

Kolhapur Sports Club is a charitable sports club registered with Charitable Commission Kolhapur. The club is formed by a team of athletes and triathletes in the year 2017 we are mostly ranging from the age group of 18 to 50 years old, although our youngest team athlete is 12 years old. Since the past 3 years, we have participated in various national and international marathons as well as triathlons. And in a short span of time, our team of athletes achieved titles such as Ironman and Ironman 70.3, while many others achieved podiums in national events
